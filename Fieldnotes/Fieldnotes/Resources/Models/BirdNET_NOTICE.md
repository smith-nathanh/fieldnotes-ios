# BirdNET Model Notice

Fieldnotes includes the following BirdNET v2.4 model artifacts:

- `BirdNET_GLOBAL_6K_V2.4_Model_FP16.tflite`
- `BirdNET_GLOBAL_6K_V2.4_MData_Model_V2_FP16.tflite`

## Fieldnotes Modification Statement

Fieldnotes distributes the listed FP16 TFLite model artifacts without further
training or modification of their weights. Fieldnotes supplies its own iOS
inference, live-audio windowing, range/season filtering, privacy filtering,
thresholding, and field-log interface around the models.
